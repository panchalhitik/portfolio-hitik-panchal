01 About me

Here's how my brain works: if something feels like a hassle, or the tool I want simply doesn't exist, I code it into existence. I enjoy taking a problem apart, understanding it properly, and building something clean and reliable in its place. Problems don't get to stay problems for long around me.

Professionally, I spent 1.5 years at NSEIT engineering high-stakes clearing systems for the National Stock Exchange of India: architecting a monolith-to-REST migration, leading delivery of a core clearing module, and mentoring junior developers. In an environment where markets are watching, I learned to build for reliability first and treat production like it deserves respect.

Academically, I'm a Computer Science Master's student at Universität Bonn (Grade: 1.7), specialising in Machine Learning, NLP and Large Language Models, from LoRA fine-tuning transformer models to building multilingual classifiers with RoBERTa and LLaMA.

And when I'm not coding? I'm watching football (huge Manchester United fan 🔴), travelling, or gaming, especially Football Manager, where I take squad-building far too seriously.
